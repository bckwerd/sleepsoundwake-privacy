SleepSoundWake — Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 16, 2026

SleepSoundWake is built to respect your privacy. This policy explains exactly what we do (and don't) with your information.

Data we collect

None. SleepSoundWake does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data, usage data, analytics, or identifiers. The app runs entirely on your device.

Data stored on your device

The app saves your preferences (clock color, alarm time, sound mix, etc.) locally using iOS's standard UserDefaults system. This data never leaves your device.

Network access

SleepSoundWake does not make any network requests.

Notifications

If you enable the alarm, the app schedules a local notification through iOS. The notification is delivered by your device, not by any server.

Third parties

SleepSoundWake does not use any third-party SDKs, advertising, or analytics services.

Children

SleepSoundWake is safe for all ages. We collect no information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes

If this policy ever changes, the "Last updated" date above will change. Substantive changes will be noted here.
